3D Printing with Autodesk Tinkercad Course

Have you ever wanted to build 3D models, but were afraid the software was too difficult to learn? Autodesk Tinkercad will change your mind! This amazing software helps you build 3D models and even export them for 3D printing. In this course, you'll create a Minecraft character like Steve! Also, you’ll learn about adding primitives, grouping pieces, and adding holes to make awesome 3D models that have a million uses.
